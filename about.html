About
Listerous is a simple tool for creating and editing lists on-the-fly. You can share a list with others by distributing its unique URL, and revisit lists you've previously viewed on this device from the history page. Listerous is designed for full functionality in both mobile and desktop browsers.

This app is powered by Firebase, and brought to you by @man1. Please feel free to contact me with suggestions, bug reports, or anything else. You can also check out Listerous on GitHub.
